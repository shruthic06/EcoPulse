💡 Sustainability Tips

🔍Always check the fabric label — synthetic fabrics like polyester shed microplastics when washed.
♻️Look for certifications like GOTS or OEKO-TEX — they verify real sustainability claims.
👕Rewearing clothes 50+ times cuts their carbon footprint by 50%. Quality over quantity.
🌊Wash synthetic clothes in a microfiber-catching bag to reduce ocean plastic pollution.
🏷️Beware of vague terms like "eco-friendly" or "green" — without certifications, they're often greenwashing.
